The Space

Californian artist Ali Sabet (sabet.art) has created a transformitive art mural masterpiece that brings contemporary design to a whole new audience– the Airbnb community. The expressive space features an edgy vibe and contemporary theme while incorporating traditional Japanese design elements such as a lime-plaster shikkui wall, polished doma concrete flooring, and a custom built kitchen/desk to compliment the decor and maximize the layout of the smaller Japanese-sized space. The condo is well-equipped with all the essentials. Guests will also enjoy the brand new foreigner-friendly bathroom. If you’re traveling with a friend, the roomy twin bunk-bed is the perfect setup for two!

Location

Dogenzaka is a vibrant & hip entertainment district in the very heart of Shibuya. Right outside the front door is an area renowned for its cutting-edge Japanese youth fashion culture and nightlife. The best part of this condo is the location; it’s literally the closest residential building to the bustling center of Shibuya. Shibuya Station is a short 4 min walk away and the best jump-off spot for exploring the rest of Tokyo by the expansive subway network. Famous landmarks such as the Shibuya Scramble Crossing, the bizarre Shibuya 109 fashion store, Meiji and Hachiko Shrines, Harajuku, and the ever-popular Yoyogi Park are all within close walking distance. If you’re a foodie, love shopping, or are looking to meet people and explore your spontaneous or adventurous side, Dogenzaka is where you want to be!
